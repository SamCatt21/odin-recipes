Chili Con Carne

Description

This warming Chile Con Carne will be a household favourite particularly on cold winter nights. It is easy to make and low on spice, making it suitable for all the family.

Ingredients

  • 500g Beef Mince (atleast 10% fat)
  • 1 finely diced large onion
  • 3 cloves crushed garlic
  • 1 finely chopped red chili (optional)
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin red kidney beans
  • 500ml beef stock
  • 2 tablespoons tomato puree
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon mild chile powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon neutral oil
