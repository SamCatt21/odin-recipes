Chili Con Carne

Description

This warming Chile Con Carne will be a household favourite particularly on cold winter nights. It is easy to make and low on spice, making it suitable for all the family.

Serve this alongside with rice, grated cheese, sour cream & guacamole.

Ingredients

  • 500g Beef Mince (atleast 10% fat)
  • 1 finely diced large onion
  • 3 cloves crushed garlic
  • 1 finely chopped red chili (optional)
  • 1 tin chopped tomatoes
  • 1 tin red kidney beans
  • 500ml beef stock
  • 2 tablespoons tomato puree
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon mild chile powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon neutral oil

Steps

  1. Preheat a medium sized pot on a high heat and add the oil.
  2. Once hot, add the beef mince and fry until browned.
  3. Remove beef from the pot (retaining the fat) and add the onion.
  4. Fry, stirring regularly, for 5 minutes or until soft and slightly caramelised.
  5. Add the garlic and chile (if using) and fry for 2 minutes or until fragrant.
  6. Put in all the ground spices and fry for 1 minute stirring constantly to avoid them burning.
  7. Stir through the tomato paste and cook for 3 minutes to remove the bitterness.
  8. Add the chopped tomatoes, kidney beans and beef stock and bring to the boil.
  9. Lower to a simmer and allow to cook for at least 30 minutes (the longer the better). Add extra water if it begins to become dry.
  10. Season to taste and serve.
