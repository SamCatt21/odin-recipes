Egg Fried Rice

Description

This quick and easy recipe is a great side dish or as a main course when additional protein is added.

For an extra kick top with sriracha or sweet chili sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500g uncooked jasmine rice
  • 4 whisked medium eggs
  • 100g frozen peas
  • 5 sliced spring onions (keep white and green parts separate)
  • 1 peeled and finely diced carrot
  • 200g cooked protein of choice (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons soya sauce
  • 1 teaspoon rice wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon neutral oil

Steps

  1. Cook rice as per packet instructions. Once cooked, spread into a thin layer on a baking tray and place in the fridge until cold.
  2. Heat a wok over a high heat. Once smoking add the oil.
  3. Once the oil is hot add the frozen peas, carrot and the white parts of the spring onion. Stir constantly for 3 minutes.
  4. Push the vegetables to one side and add the egg and the protein (if using). Continue to stir until the egg is cooked.
  5. Mix everything together and then add the rice. Continue to stir until the rice is fully reheated.
  6. Add the soya sauce, rice wine vinegar and oyster suace and stir until fully mixed through.
  7. Serve topping with the green parts of the spring onions.
